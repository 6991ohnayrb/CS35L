Description of SubWindows in Gitk

top left
  shows the tree and branches of repository
  we can see where commits were made and merges made
top middle
  we see the author of the corresponding commit
top right
  we see the date and time of the commit
  YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM:SS
bottom left
  we see more information about the author and branches
  if the selected is not a merge, we see change logs as well
bottom right
  we see additional comments made by the author
